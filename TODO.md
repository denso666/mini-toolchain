## Commands

> < `v` | `c` | `u` | `n` > _command_
> - `v` Verify
> - `c` Completed
> - `u` Uncompleted
> - `n` Not implemented

- [`c`] uname
- [`v`] ls
- [`c`] cat
- [`c`] lsmod
- [`c`] uptime
- [`c`] mkdir
- [`v`] rm [-r]
- [`c`] chown
- [`c`] chmod
- [`c`] touch
- [`c`] kill
- [`n`] pidof
- [`n`] killall
- [`n`] cp
